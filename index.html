Tournament Rules

Format & Rules

This tournament will follow the Wizards EDH rules (Tier 5) and the official banlist as defined by cEDH Europe.

The tournament will consist of Swiss rounds, followed by a top-4 or top-10 playoff, depending on the number of players.

Players will earn 5 points for each win. If time runs out without a winner, the match will end immediately, and all players—including those eliminated—will receive 1 point.

Proxy Policy

This event is proxy-friendly with the following requirements:

  • Proxies must be printed in color
  • Artwork must be recognizable
  • No paper slips in front of cards

If you have questions about specific proxies, please contact the organizers before the event.

Prizes

100% of entry fees will be distributed as prize pool in the form of vouchers redeemable at the event's official vendors.

The tournament winner will receive a ticket to compete at the European Championship in Munich!

Prize distribution:

  • 1st Place: 30% of the prize pool, which includes the Qualification ticket.
  • 2nd Place: 20% of prize pool
  • 3rd-4th Place: 15% of prize pool each
  • 5th-8th Place: 5% of prize pool each

Additional prizes from sponsors may be announced closer to the event date.
